## Top 2 Selected

### Candidate-024
Most feature-rich of the three. The TOKEN TEMP dropdown actually controls bet variance and risk, not just cosmetic. Has a live terminal log, immersive AI-themed UI, and the AGI progress bar adds a layer of gameplay that the others don't have.

### Candidate-034
Most polished casino mechanics. The blur-on-spin effect feels smooth, the full paytable with AI-themed outcomes is well done, and the green terminal aesthetic is the most cohesive visual identity of the bunch. Simpler than 024 but more consistently executed.

## Candidate Dropped

### Candidate-020
Good branding and the shaking loss animation was fun, but the overall feature set fell behind the other two after refinement. The funny footer details weren't enough to make up for less gameplay depth and fewer interactive elements compared to 024 and 034.

## Observations

After two refinement rounds, the candidates started to diverge more. 024 leaned into complexity (TOKEN TEMP as a real mechanic, AGI bar, more symbols). 034 leaned into polish (smoother animations, tighter aesthetic). 020 didn't move forward as much from its Step 2 version. The refinement prompt helped candidates that already had strong foundations more than it helped the weaker one.
